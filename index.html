About

Welcome, my name is Dustin and I currently am located in the Vancouver Washington area. Prior to living in the Great North West I resided in Los Angeles California for one year, were I obtained my education for Web Development. I completed General Assembly's Web Development Immersive WDI course along with Thinkful's FEWD course, all which was done in a six month period. During my course term I received a vital piece of advice from a guest speaker who said “The key to success in this business is to stay current on languages”. Since then I have been digesting all my brain can allow with the help of Code Academy, Team Treehouse, and Freecodecamp.com. Not to mention the pile of JavaScript and jQuery books I have on my desk.

What an amazing experience adult education is, discovering so much about your abilities and ways to offer yourself again. Sharing my knowledge and collaborating with others is one aspect in this field I very much embrace. I have been fortunate enough to mentor other classmates and fellow coders within forums and chatrooms, realizing where my passions lie. Every day it is something new and challenging since technology is ever changing, and to have the capability to offer a solution is such a incredible benefit to being a web developer.

Away from the keyboard I tend to flock towards hobbies that are hands on building to photography, or even some activities that could potentially be fatal (i.e., Flying planes, motorcycle stunts, welding........marrying a Gemini). But spending time with my family and exploring our new surrounding is by far the best hobby of them all.
